Comments

The Failover State metric is mapped:

 

Normal: 2

Warning: 4

Failed: 1, 3

 

1: Dead

2: Can Takeover

3: Cannot Takeover

4: Takeover

 

The Failover Settings metric is mapped:

 

Normal: 2

Failed: 1, 3, 4, 5

 

1: Not Configured

2: Enabled

3: Disabled

4: Takeover by Partner Disabled

5: This Node Dead

 

The Failover Partner Status metric is mapped:

 

Normal: 1

Failed: 2-6

 

1: OK

2: Unknown Reason

3: Disabled by Operator

4: Interconnect Offline

5: Disable by Partner

6: Takeover Failed

 

The Interconnect Status metric is mapped:

 

Normal: 4

Failed: 1-3

 

1: Not Present

2: Down

3: Partial Failure

4: Up
